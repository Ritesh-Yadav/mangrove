Create your Questionnaire

1. You want answers to key questions for your program

DataWinners helps you monitor and evaluate your program's progress.

With DataWinners anyone can collect data. It is designed for program managers, even those with little or no Information Technology (IT) experience.

See our Success Stories »

2. Choose your key questions

Sit together with your program team to determine the key questions.

3. Create your Questionnaire

Follow DataWinners' simple step-by-step instructions to turn your key questions into an online Questionnaire.

Test your Questionnaire by sending in sample answers.

4. Train your Data Senders

Train your field staff (Data Senders) to send in their answers.

DataWinners makes it easy; print out and share a copy of your Questionnaire with your Data Senders.

Receive accurate Data

1. Data Senders send in their answers

Your Data Senders (field staff) fill out the Questionnaire on paper, then use the completed questionnaire as a guide to send their answers via

  • SMS
  • Internet

2. Improve the quality of your data

DataWinners will communicate with your Data Senders automatically:

  • Positive feedback
    "Congrats Shilpa! We successfully received your data"
  • Error message
    "Error. Please send your answers for question 1 and 5 again."

Read more »

3. Get your data on time

Tell DataWinners how often and when you need your data.

DataWinners will send automatic reminders to your Data Senders:

"Don't forget, your monthly report is due today."
