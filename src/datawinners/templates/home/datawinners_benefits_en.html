WHAT IS DATAWINNERS

  • How it Works
  • Benefits

Use DataWinners to get a complete, accurate and timely set of answers to your key questions.

Here’s why you and your organization should use DataWinners:


1. Start Today

  • Try DataWinners’ full functionality completely free
  • Sign up in minutes. Start collecting data the same day
  • Access DataWinners online from your own computer - No software installation or special equipment required
  • Get everything you need as part of your monthly subscription: a telephone number, SMS packet and unlimited data collection potential

2. Save Money

  • Collect your data for one low monthly fee
  • Pay-as-you-go, No long term contracts or hidden fees
  • Sign up for one subscription for your entire organization; Each manager can collect data for his/her project
  • Keep costs down; field staff use their own telephones to SMS data
  • Collect an unlimited amount of data
  • No expensive consultants or programmers needed

3. Build tools to meet your needs

  • Designed for managers; No prior IT experience required
  • Do-it-yourself mobile data collection; Design your project with DataWinners step-by-step instructions
  • Fully Customizable; Build data collection and communication tools that fit your exact needs
  • Easily edit existing questionnaires or build new ones as needs change

4. Improve lines of communication

  • Easy to use: Build your questionnaire in the morning and that afternoon start collecting your data
  • Choose the channel that works for you: SMS, Internet, or both; DataWinners will help you decide
  • Collect data nationwide, even in remote areas
  • Receive data from the field in seconds; watch it arrive in real-time on your computer screen
  • Set criteria to improve accuracy
  • Send automatic reminders, confirmations and specific error messages to your data senders

5. Work Collaboratively

  • Share your data with stakeholders across town or on the other side of the world
  • Be part of an active open-source community that contributes new features and code to DataWinners all the time

6. Feel Confident

  • Access your data from any internet-connected computer in the world, 24/7
  • Your data is encrypted and password protected; even our DataWinners administrators don’t have access.
  • Your data is stored on secure servers and regularly backed up.
  • You own your data – export it whenever you like and take it with you

7. Gain Insight and Make Better Decisions

  • Filter to find the exact answer you need
  • Analyze your data right away using DataWinners mapping and spreadsheet tools
  • Export your data to Excel in one click for further analysis
