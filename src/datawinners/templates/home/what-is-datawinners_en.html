DataWinners is an online mobile data collection service designed and built for development professionals. With DataWinners, you collect and analyze the data you need using the equipment you already have – a computer, the Internet and basic mobile phones.

DataWinners’ goal is to help you

Open lines of communication between you at headquarters and field staff/beneficiaries.

 

Get a better understanding of your project’s current situation.

 

You can make informed decisions.
